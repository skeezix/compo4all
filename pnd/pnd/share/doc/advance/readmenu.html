AdvanceMENU Frontend

AdvanceMENU is a frontend for the AdvanceMAME, AdvanceMESS, MAME, MESS, xmame, Raine and any other emulator.

It runs in Linux, Mac OS X, DOS, Windows and in all the other platforms supported by the SDL library.

The official site of AdvanceMENU is :

http://advancemame.sourceforge.net/

This package contains :
advmenu The frontend.
advcfg The automatic video configurator.
advv The manual video configurator and tester.
